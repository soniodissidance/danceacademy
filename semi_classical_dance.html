Semi Classical Dance

Get In Touch

Odissi Semi-Classical Dance blends the traditional grace and technique of classical Odissi with elements of lighter, more contemporary music and performance styles. While it retains the expressive storytelling, rhythm, and gestures of classical Odissi, semi-classical dance allows for more flexibility, creativity, and fusion, making it accessible to a wider audience.
