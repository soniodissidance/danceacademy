Soni Dance Academy is dedicated to preserving and promoting Indian classical heritage through the teaching of Odissi dance. Our classes are well-structured, engaging, and inclusive, with a strong focus on building strong fundamentals and refining each movement to support every dancer’s overall growth, under the guidance of Guru Sunita Rout and a committed faculty team. Students are carefully guided and empowered with the knowledge, skills, and techniques needed to understand cultural and social contexts, while also developing positive life skills that help them confidently navigate cross-cultural environments.

Guru Sunita Rout is a highly respected Odissi classical dancer, choreographer, and teacher with over 25 years of experience. As the Founder and Director of Soni Dance Academy, she is known for her graceful performances, innovative choreography, and dedicated teaching. She is deeply committed to preserving the classical essence of Odissi while nurturing the next generation with discipline, devotion, creativity, and cultural pride.
